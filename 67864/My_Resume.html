What can I do

Here are my types of art that I am doing. I'm still exploring more art styles in my work.

Semi Realistic

I mostly do this art style on portraits. It is fully colored with the details. This style usually takes 1 hour to finish depending on the complexity

Line art

This type of art style mostly will be doing on a white background for minimalism. There will be no gradations in shade or hue. It takes around 30 minutes to finish simple ones.

Cartoon

Cartoon art styles are simple with not many details in them. I usually do it colorful to make it live. This can take around 15 minutes to finish one simple character.
