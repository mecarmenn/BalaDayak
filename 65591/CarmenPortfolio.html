Early Childhood Education

Final Year Project: GraphoGame


Having 2 younger siblings at home made me realized how much I love to be around them as they are my bundle of joy. It broke my heart to know that some of the children have difficulties in reading. Therefore, I did a comprehensive review for my Final Year Project 1 related to this issue. The title of my FYP is 'A Comprehensive Review: The Psychology Behind the Reward System in GraphoGame'. In my FYP, I want to find out how the rewards help children with dyslexia to read and if it could motivate them into achieving more.

Make-up & Beauty

BeauBoss of BEAUTYRA


We all want to look good for ourselves and for others. Ellen Sinkman, author of the book The Psychology of Beauty, says the desire to be beautiful is programmed into us. Therefore, there is no harm in using makeup to enhance our features to improve our overall appearance, which will also boost our confidence. I've been a dropshipper and personal shopper for local cosmetic brand BEAUTYRA for almost 3 months now.
